Loid Forger

Loid Forger (ロイド・フォージャー, Roido Fōjā) is the main protagonist of the SPY x FAMILY series. He has discarded everything about his past to work for his country as an undercover spy who acts under the codename "Twilight" (〈黄昏たそがれ〉).
For his current mission, he is required to marry and have a child, thus he enters a marriage with Yor Briar and becomes the adoptive father of Anya Forger.
