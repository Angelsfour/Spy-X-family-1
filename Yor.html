Yor Forger

Yor Forger (ヨル・フォージャー, Yoru Fōjā?), née Briar (ブライア, Buraia) is the tritagonist of the SPY x FAMILY series. While she works as an ordinary clerk at Berlint City Hall, she also leads a secret life as a Garden assassin with the code name "Thorn Princess" (〈いばら姫ひめ〉).
She and Loid Forger get married to fulfil their separate goals, with the former becoming the adoptive mother of Anya Forger.
